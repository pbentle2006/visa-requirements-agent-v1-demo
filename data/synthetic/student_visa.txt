
International Student Visa (S3)
Immigration Instructions

Effective Date: 1 January 2024
Version: 1.4

OBJECTIVE
The International Student Visa visa supports international education and cultural exchange.

SCOPE
This instruction applies to applications for International Student Visa visas lodged on or after the effective date, whether inside or outside the country.


ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

S3.5 Basic Requirements
An applicant for a International Student Visa visa must:
(c) hold adequate health insurance;
(a) be enrolled in an approved course;
(c) meet English language requirements;
(b) demonstrate financial capacity;
(d) satisfy the genuine student test;

S3.10 Specific Requirements
(a) demonstrate academic progression;
(b) maintain full-time enrollment;
(c) limit work to permitted hours;


FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS

S3.15 Financial Support
Applicants must demonstrate access to funds of at least $25,000 or equivalent.
Acceptable evidence includes:
(a) bank statements for the last 3 months;
(b) employment letter with salary details;
(c) sponsorship undertaking with financial evidence.

S3.20 Income Thresholds
No specific income thresholds apply.


HEALTH AND CHARACTER

S3.25 Health Requirements
All applicants must meet health requirements including:
(a) medical examination by approved panel physician;
(b) chest X-ray examination;
(c) HIV test if required.

S3.30 Character Requirements
All applicants must meet character requirements including:
(a) police clearance certificates from all countries of residence;
(b) declaration of criminal history;
(c) assessment of risk to community safety.


VISA CONDITIONS

S3.35 Standard Conditions
(a) Maintain full-time enrollment
(b) Work limited to 20 hours per week
(c) Maintain adequate health insurance

S3.40 Special Conditions
(a) Restriction on travel to certain areas
(b) Participation in integration programs


PROCESSING REQUIREMENTS

S3.45 Application Process
Applications must be lodged:
(a) online through the official portal;
(b) with all required documentation;
(c) accompanied by the prescribed fee;
(d) before the applicant's current visa expires (if applicable).

S3.50 Decision Criteria
Decisions will be made based on:
(a) satisfaction of all eligibility requirements;
(b) genuineness of application;
(c) risk assessment outcomes.
