
Skilled Worker Visa (W4)
Immigration Instructions

Effective Date: 1 January 2024
Version: 2.0

OBJECTIVE
The Skilled Worker Visa visa enables skilled workers to contribute to the economy.

SCOPE
This instruction applies to applications for Skilled Worker Visa visas lodged on or after the effective date, whether inside or outside the country.


ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

W4.5 Basic Requirements
An applicant for a Skilled Worker Visa visa must:
(a) have a genuine job offer from an approved employer;
(d) meet health requirements;
(f) have relevant qualifications;
(c) meet English language requirements;
(e) meet character requirements;
(b) have their skills assessed by the relevant authority;

W4.10 Specific Requirements
(a) demonstrate relevant work experience;
(b) meet salary threshold requirements;
(c) employer must be approved sponsor;
(d) undergo additional assessment;
(e) provide supplementary documentation;
(f) attend interview if required;


FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS

W4.15 Financial Support
Applicants must demonstrate access to funds of at least $20,000 or equivalent.
Acceptable evidence includes:
(a) bank statements for the last 3 months;
(b) employment letter with salary details;
(c) sponsorship undertaking with financial evidence.

W4.20 Income Thresholds
Single applicant: $45,000 per annum
Couple: $55,000 per annum
Family with dependents: $65,000 per annum


HEALTH AND CHARACTER

W4.25 Health Requirements
All applicants must meet health requirements including:
(a) medical examination by approved panel physician;
(b) chest X-ray examination;
(c) HIV test if required.
(d) additional specialist examinations if indicated;
(e) health insurance covering pre-existing conditions.

W4.30 Character Requirements
All applicants must meet character requirements including:
(a) police clearance certificates from all countries of residence;
(b) declaration of criminal history;
(c) assessment of risk to community safety.


VISA CONDITIONS

W4.35 Standard Conditions
(a) Work only for approved employer
(b) Notify of change in circumstances
(c) Maintain adequate health insurance

W4.40 Special Conditions
(a) Restriction on travel to certain areas
(b) Electronic monitoring if required


PROCESSING REQUIREMENTS

W4.45 Application Process
Applications must be lodged:
(a) online through the official portal;
(b) with all required documentation;
(c) accompanied by the prescribed fee;
(d) before the applicant's current visa expires (if applicable).

W4.50 Decision Criteria
Decisions will be made based on:
(a) satisfaction of all eligibility requirements;
(b) genuineness of application;
(c) risk assessment outcomes.
(d) public interest considerations;
(e) ministerial direction compliance;
(f) precedent case analysis.
