
Family Reunion Visa (F1)
Immigration Instructions

Effective Date: 1 January 2024
Version: 5.9

OBJECTIVE
The Family Reunion Visa visa facilitates family reunification and support.

SCOPE
This instruction applies to applications for Family Reunion Visa visas lodged on or after the effective date, whether inside or outside the country.


ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

F1.5 Basic Requirements
An applicant for a Family Reunion Visa visa must:
(d) meet health requirements;
(a) provide evidence of relationship;
(b) have sufficient funds for their stay;
(d) meet accommodation requirements;
(b) have an eligible sponsor;
(e) meet character requirements;

F1.10 Specific Requirements
(a) demonstrate ongoing relationship;
(b) meet sponsorship obligations;
(c) satisfy integration requirements;


FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS

F1.15 Financial Support
Applicants must demonstrate access to funds of at least $25,000 or equivalent.
Acceptable evidence includes:
(a) bank statements for the last 3 months;
(b) employment letter with salary details;
(c) sponsorship undertaking with financial evidence.

F1.20 Income Thresholds
Single applicant: $65,000 per annum
Couple: $85,000 per annum
Family with dependents: $105,000 per annum


HEALTH AND CHARACTER

F1.25 Health Requirements
All applicants must meet health requirements including:
(a) medical examination by approved panel physician;
(b) chest X-ray examination;
(c) HIV test if required.

F1.30 Character Requirements
All applicants must meet character requirements including:
(a) police clearance certificates from all countries of residence;
(b) declaration of criminal history;
(c) assessment of risk to community safety.


VISA CONDITIONS

F1.35 Standard Conditions
(a) Comply with sponsorship arrangements
(b) Notify of change in circumstances
(c) Maintain adequate health insurance

F1.40 Special Conditions
(a) Regular reporting to authorities
(b) Participation in integration programs


PROCESSING REQUIREMENTS

F1.45 Application Process
Applications must be lodged:
(a) online through the official portal;
(b) with all required documentation;
(c) accompanied by the prescribed fee;
(d) before the applicant's current visa expires (if applicable).

F1.50 Decision Criteria
Decisions will be made based on:
(a) satisfaction of all eligibility requirements;
(b) genuineness of application;
(c) risk assessment outcomes.
