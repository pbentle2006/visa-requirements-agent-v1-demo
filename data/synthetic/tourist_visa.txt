
Tourist Visa (V1)
Immigration Instructions

Effective Date: 1 January 2024
Version: 2.6

OBJECTIVE
The Tourist Visa visa allows temporary entry for tourism, business, or family visits.

SCOPE
This instruction applies to applications for Tourist Visa visas lodged on or after the effective date, whether inside or outside the country.


ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

V1.5 Basic Requirements
An applicant for a Tourist Visa visa must:
(d) have confirmed accommodation arrangements;
(a) hold a passport valid for at least 6 months;
(e) hold adequate travel insurance;

V1.10 Specific Requirements
(a) demonstrate genuine intention to visit temporarily;
(b) show ties to home country;
(c) not intend to work or study;


FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS

V1.15 Financial Support
Applicants must demonstrate access to funds of at least $10,000 or equivalent.
Acceptable evidence includes:
(a) bank statements for the last 3 months;
(b) employment letter with salary details;
(c) sponsorship undertaking with financial evidence.

V1.20 Income Thresholds
No specific income thresholds apply.


HEALTH AND CHARACTER

V1.25 Health Requirements
All applicants must meet health requirements including:
(a) medical examination by approved panel physician;
(b) chest X-ray examination;
(c) HIV test if required.

V1.30 Character Requirements
All applicants must meet character requirements including:
(a) police clearance certificates from all countries of residence;
(b) declaration of criminal history;
(c) assessment of risk to community safety.


VISA CONDITIONS

V1.35 Standard Conditions
(a) No work permitted
(b) No study for more than 3 months
(c) Must maintain adequate health insurance

V1.40 Special Conditions
No special conditions apply.


PROCESSING REQUIREMENTS

V1.45 Application Process
Applications must be lodged:
(a) online through the official portal;
(b) with all required documentation;
(c) accompanied by the prescribed fee;
(d) before the applicant's current visa expires (if applicable).

V1.50 Decision Criteria
Decisions will be made based on:
(a) satisfaction of all eligibility requirements;
(b) genuineness of application;
(c) risk assessment outcomes.
