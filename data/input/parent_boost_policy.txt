PARENT BOOST VISITOR VISA (V4)

V4.1 OBJECTIVE

The Parent Boost Visitor Visa is intended to:
(a) Enable family reunification by allowing parents of skilled migrants to visit New Zealand
(b) Support New Zealand's labour force by helping retain skilled migrants
(c) Improve settlement outcomes for skilled migrants and their families
(d) Provide economic benefits through visitor spending

V4.5 INSTRUCTIONS

V4.5(a) General Requirements
(i) The applicant must be outside New Zealand when the application is made and when the visa is granted
(ii) The applicant must hold a valid passport
(iii) The applicant must be sponsored by an eligible sponsor

V4.10 SPONSORSHIP REQUIREMENTS

V4.10(a) Sponsor Eligibility
The sponsor must:
(i) Be a New Zealand citizen or resident
(ii) Be the adult child of the applicant
(iii) Have completed the Parent Boost Sponsorship Form (INZ 1234)

V4.10(f) Sponsor Limitations
(i) A maximum of two sponsors may support one application
(ii) A sponsor may sponsor a maximum of six parents (including partners)

V4.15 HEALTH REQUIREMENTS

V4.15(a) The applicant must meet health requirements equivalent to residence standard
V4.15(b) Medical certificates must be:
(i) Completed by an INZ-approved panel physician
(ii) Current at the time of application (not older than 3 months)
(iii) Valid for 36 months from date of issue

V4.20 CHARACTER REQUIREMENTS

V4.20(a) The applicant must be of good character
V4.20(b) Police certificates required from:
(i) Country of citizenship
(ii) Any country where applicant has lived for 12+ months in last 10 years

V4.25 FINANCIAL REQUIREMENTS

V4.25.10 Income Thresholds
The sponsor(s) must demonstrate minimum income over the last 3 tax years:
(a) For 1-2 parents: NZD $65,000 per year
(b) For 3-4 parents: NZD $85,000 per year
(c) For 5-6 parents: NZD $105,000 per year

V4.25.15 Income Verification
(i) Income must be verified by Inland Revenue
(ii) Must include salary, wages, and self-employment income
(iii) Does not include benefits, allowances, or investment income

V4.25.20 Maintenance Funds
The applicant must have access to:
(a) NZD $10,000 for principal applicant
(b) NZD $5,000 for each additional family member

V4.30 INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

V4.30(a) The applicant must hold comprehensive travel and medical insurance
V4.30(b) Insurance must:
(i) Cover the entire duration of stay
(ii) Minimum coverage of NZD $200,000
(iii) Be from an approved insurance provider

V4.35 DEPENDENT CHILDREN

V4.35(a) Dependent children may be included if:
(i) Under 18 years of age
(ii) Unmarried and without partner
(iii) Not supporting their own children

V4.40 PARTNERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

V4.40(a) Partners may be included if:
(i) In a genuine and stable relationship
(ii) Living together for at least 12 months
(iii) Relationship is recognized under New Zealand law

V4.45 VISA CONDITIONS

V4.45(a) Maximum stay: 3 years
V4.45(b) No work rights
V4.45(c) No access to publicly funded health services
V4.45(d) Must maintain insurance throughout stay
V4.45(e) Must depart New Zealand before visa expires

V4.50 DECLINE REASONS

Applications may be declined if:
(a) Applicant is in New Zealand at time of application or grant
(b) Sponsorship requirements not met
(c) Health requirements not met
(d) Character requirements not met
(e) Financial requirements not met
(f) Insurance requirements not met
(g) Information provided is false or misleading
(h) Previous visa conditions breached
